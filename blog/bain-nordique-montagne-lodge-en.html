The apartment

The Nordic bath facing the pines: the Lodge's signature experience

By Lodge Le Chamois · July 05, 2026

Some details turn a stay into a lasting memory. At Lodge Le Chamois, it is undoubtedly our wooden Nordic bath, set on the 45 sqm south-facing terrace, facing the pines and the mountains.

What is a Nordic bath?

A Nordic bath is a wooden hot-water tub, traditionally heated, in which you relax in the open air. Unlike a conventional spa, it blends into its surroundings: here, the water gently steams while your gaze drifts into the forest. After a day of skiing or hiking, the effect on your muscles — and your mind — is immediate.

A terrace designed for togetherness

Our terrace is much more than the bath. South-facing, it becomes a true open-air lounge opening onto a green garden. You'll find a fire bowl to extend the evenings, a barbecue for grilling, a table and benches for meals, and sun loungers to enjoy the sunshine. In summer as in winter, it is the heart of the apartment.

A real "lodge", more than an apartment

A lodge is a characterful place designed for an immersive experience: authentic materials, wood and stone, spaces open to nature. More intimate than a hotel, more exclusive than an ordinary apartment, it invites you to experience the mountains differently — in comfort, calm and a warm atmosphere.

Good to know

The Nordic bath is available as an option and heated on request: simply let us know ahead of your stay. We take care of everything so it is at temperature when you arrive.
